- Prompt-to-diagram generation that outputs draw.io-compatible nodes and edges.
- Built-in editor for drag-and-drop adjustments, colors, and grouping.
- Imports and exports `.drawio`, PNG, and SVG files for sharing.
- Configurable model providers via environment variables to keep data private.
- Deploys on Docker, Vercel, or any Next.js-capable host.
